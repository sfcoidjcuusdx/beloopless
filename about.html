About Us

LoopLess is dedicated to helping people reduce screen time and reclaim focus, purpose, and emotional balance. Our tools are based on Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) and behavioral science.

We believe in a healthier digital lifestyle — one that’s intentional, mindful, and empowering. Whether you’re trying to quit doomscrolling or just find more balance, we’ve got your back.
